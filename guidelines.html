Safety Guidelines

Flood Safety

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Avoid walking through floodwater.
  • Keep emergency supplies ready.
  • Follow official alerts.

Earthquake Safety

  • Drop, Cover and Hold On.
  • Stay away from windows.
  • Move to open areas after shaking stops.
  • Check for injuries.

Cyclone Safety

  • Stay indoors.
  • Secure doors and windows.
  • Prepare emergency kits.
  • Evacuate if instructed.

Fire Safety

  • Use nearest safe exit.
  • Do not use elevators.
  • Call emergency services.
  • Stay low if smoke is present.

Landslide Safety

  • Move away from steep slopes immediately.
  • Watch for falling rocks and debris.
  • Follow evacuation instructions.
  • Avoid affected roads and pathways.

Heatwave Safety

  • Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Avoid outdoor activities during peak heat.
  • Wear light-colored clothing.
  • Never leave children or pets in vehicles.

Lightning Safety

  • Seek shelter inside a building.
  • Avoid open fields and tall trees.
  • Stay away from electrical appliances.
  • Wait at least 30 minutes after the last thunder.

General Emergency

  • Keep emergency contacts readily available.
  • Prepare a disaster emergency kit.
  • Follow instructions from local authorities.
  • Stay calm and help others if safe.
